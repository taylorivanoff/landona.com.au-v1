Services

Residential

Rural properties to beach-front holiday getaways, our range of NSW residential property conveyancing experience is vast. Our residential services include preparing and overseeing Contracts, due diligence required, ordering specialized property certificates and more.

Strata

We review Strata Contracts and order Strata reports. Strata reports provide the full financial history, future expenditure and current financial position of a Strata corporation. We’ll also make you aware of by-laws changes to protect your rights of property.

Off-the-plan

We’ll be by your side every step of the way, from reviewing Strata plans, to fighting Vendor’s special conditions and more. As you’re buying the “concept” of a building, having experienced conveyancers detail through the Contract is vital to your success.

Company Title

Applicable to both strata and off-the-plan, company titles are directed through reviewing company agreements, auditing the memorandum of articles and investigating the reports’ provided by the managing agent of the property.

Contract Reviews

Landona Conveyancing can provide a comprehensive analysis of the Contract to you for free. This analysis includes: auditing special conditions, devising a suitable settlement date, examining easements and covenants, reviewing council disclosure documents for zoning, inspecting neighbouring zones and building development proposals and more.

Transfer

Land and property transfer between family or executors can be achieved through our services. After receiving client instructions, Landona Conveyancing will communicate with the NSW Land Registry Services and submit the appropriate documents on behalf.
